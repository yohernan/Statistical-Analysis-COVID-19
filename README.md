#	COVID-19 Excess Casualties Statistical Analysis (RStudio): 

Conducted hypothesis testing in RStudio to analyze excess mortality associated with COVID‑19.

Performed data cleaning, exploratory analysis, and statistical modeling; obtained a p‑value of 0.0005994, leading to rejection of the null hypothesis at the 5% significance level.

Communicated findings through visualizations and statistical summaries to support data‑driven interpretation of public health trends.
